ABOUT US

BTKIT is an autonomous institute of the Uttarakhand Government affiliated to Uttarakhand Technial University, Dehradun. Havng been established in 1991, BTKIT Dwarahat has a strong base of alumni spread all around the world. The BTKIT Computing Club works in the college and is maintained by the students undergoing the courses of B.Tech and MCA in the institute. Our club is responsible for conducting all technical activities of the college under the Computer Science Department. The club conducts workshop, guest lectures, placement oriented talks, aptitude tests etc in the institute. The club is responsible for the interaction among juniors and seniors in the college and also connecting to the Alumni related to Computer Science and IT Sector. The computing club also maintains a discussion forum for the community of students-alumni so that interactions among them happens smoothly and one can easily get solution to his/her problem.
